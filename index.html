CUSTOMER CHURN ANALYSIS

Tools and Technologies:

  • Tableau - visualization tool

Customer churn analysis is a data-driven approach that helps businesses understand and predict customer destruction. By analyzing customer data and identifying churn indicators, companies can develop strategies to retain valuable customers, reduce costs, enhance customer experiences, and drive revenue growth. This analysis enables companies to proactively address churn risks, develop targeted retention strategies, and optimize customer experiences. By reducing churn rates, businesses can enhance customer loyalty, reduce costs associated with customer acquisition, and drive sustainable growth. Customer churn analysis empowers businesses to make informed decisions and take proactive measures to foster long-term customer loyalty.

HANDWRITTEN DIGIT RECOGNITION

Tools and Technologies:

  • Anaconda - Jupyter Notebook
  • Python

Handwritten digit recognition using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) is a technique that utilizes deep learning to accurately classify handwritten digits. By extracting features from images and training the model, it can predict the digit represented in a given image. This approach has widespread applications and has significantly improved the accuracy of digit recognition tasks. Some of the real world applications includes OCR, postal automation, CAPTCHA solving, bank check processing, forms processing, medical records analysis, digital assistants, data entry automation, machine vision, and education/research.

CUBIC ZIRCONIA - PREDICTION

Tools and Technologies:

  • Anaconda - Jupyter Notebook
  • Python

Cubic zirconia prediction is the use of machine learning algorithms to estimate the quality and attributes of cubic zirconia gemstones. By analyzing data on various gemstone features such as color, clarity, cut, carat weight, and more, predictive models can be developed to provide accurate estimations of cubic zirconia's quality. These predictions assist consumers in making informed purchasing decisions and benefit the gemstone industry by facilitating pricing, inventory management, and quality control processes. Overall, cubic zirconia prediction enhances the understanding and evaluation of these gemstones, improving transparency and efficiency in the market.
